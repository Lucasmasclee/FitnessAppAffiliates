Become an affiliate

All the details about the affiliate program and how to join.

Sign in to continue

Register with Google to become an affiliate. You’ll then fill in your contact details so we can get in touch and give you your personal link.

How much will you earn?

  • We ask €25/month, and €150/year. The playstore and appstore take 15%, which leaves us with €21.25 and €127.50 respectively.
  • For every monthly subscription, the commission is a one-time payment of €20 (94%).
  • For every yearly subscription, the commission is a one-time payment of €50 (39%).
  • You can track your subscriptions in your dashboard, and we will pay you out every 1st of the month.
  • Custom offers and partnerships are allowed, but not guaranteed.

How does the sign-up process work?

  • 1. Fill out the form below, and we will contact you within 48 hours.
  • 2. We create your personal link
  • 3. That's it, you can start sharing the link.

Track all of your stats

After we've created your personal link, you will get access to your dashboard. In your dashboard you can see all the stats of your link, such as clicks, downloads, and subscriptions.

All link data is retrieved directly from AppsFlyer.
