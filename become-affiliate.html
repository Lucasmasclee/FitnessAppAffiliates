Become an affiliate

This page explains how the program works, what you earn, and how to apply. After you submit the form, we’ll contact you within 48 hours and you’ll get access to your dashboard and your unique link.

Sign in to continue

We use Google sign-in so you have one account for your dashboard and your affiliate link. No extra password to remember.

How much you earn

90% of net revenue for the first month on every monthly subscription from your link. 50% of net revenue for the first year on every yearly subscription from your link.

Commission is paid on net revenue. The App Store and Play Store take 15% of revenue; we pay you on what we receive after that. Our prices are subject to change.

  • You can track your subscriptions in your dashboard.
  • We pay out every 1st of the month.
  • Custom offers and partnerships are allowed, but not guaranteed.

How sign-up works

  1. Submit the form below. We’ll contact you within 48 hours.
  2. Get access to your dashboard and your unique affiliate link.
  3. Start sharing. That’s it—you can begin promoting as soon as you have your link.

Tracking & dashboard

After we create your personal link, you get access to your dashboard. There you’ll see clicks, downloads, and subscriptions for your link. All link data is retrieved directly from AppsFlyer.

Affiliate attribution is determined by AppsFlyer. When multiple affiliate links are clicked, the most recent eligible click will receive attribution. Attribution windows may vary depending on platform policies and privacy settings.
