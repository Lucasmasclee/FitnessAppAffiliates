FAQ

How is LiftBetter different from other workout apps?

Other apps give you predetermined splits, predetermined volume, too much volume to recover from, too many sets, too little rest time, and recommend suboptimal exercises.

Is LiftBetter good for beginners?

Yes. The onboarding process adapts your plan to your experience level and helps you know exactly what to do in the gym. The video instructions as well as the intelligent workout planner will help you stay consistent.

Can I prioritize certain muscle groups?

Yes. You can adjust how much emphasis you want on different muscle groups in as much detail as you want and prioritize the muscles you care about most.

Can I track my workouts?

Yes. You can log sets, reps, and RIR while comparing your performance to previous workouts. You will also get to see a summary of each completed workout together with its statistics.

How long does it take to create a plan?

5-15 minutes, depending on how much personalization and optimization you want. After answering a short survey, the app will generate your personalized training plan.
